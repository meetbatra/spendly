## FAQs

### 1) Is this actually free, and why?
Yes. The audit itself is free because Spendly is designed to surface optimization opportunities quickly, then help high-savings teams execute through the broader Credex ecosystem.

### 2) How accurate are the recommendations?
Spendly uses a deterministic, rule-based engine with explicit price math and per-tool reasoning. Recommendations are based on your declared plans, seat counts, spend, and use case context.

### 3) Do you store my spending data?
If you submit an audit and generate a shareable result, Spendly stores the audit payload needed to render that report. Sensitive handling should still follow your internal policy before sharing externally.

### 4) What is Credex, and why is it mentioned?
Credex provides discounted AI credits. Spendly identifies where you could save; Credex is one execution path for teams with meaningful monthly savings opportunities.

### 5) How often should I re-audit my stack?
Re-audit whenever team size changes materially, you add/remove tools, or pricing plans change. A practical cadence is at least once per quarter.
